About

Kieran McAuliffe is an emerging musical artist working in a variety of contemporary genres. He has collaborated with major artists including Kiyoshi Kitagawa, Billy Martin, and Andy Milne. He has performed internationally at major venues including Bern Jazz Festival, the Stone, the Blue Note NYC, and the Apollo Theater. Kieran holds a BFA in Performance from the New School, is currently working on an MM in Composition from SUNY Purchase, and very soon will be pursuing a Doctorate somewhere!
